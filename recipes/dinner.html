Dinner of the day

Velvet Vista Velouté

Indulge in Silky Elegance Tonight

Velvet Vista Velouté is not just a meal but an experience for the senses. Luscious ribbons of fettuccine bathe in a decadent, creamy sauce, accentuated by the nutty undertones of parmesan and the earthy essence of white mushrooms. Every bite is like a smooth caress on the palate, inviting a moment of culinary bliss. Perfect for those evenings when only pure indulgence will do.

Working Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 250g Fettuccine pasta
  • 200ml Heavy cream
  • 100g Grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • Handful of Fresh basil leaves, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • A pinch of Ground nutmeg
  • 150g White mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
  • A pinch of Sea salt, to taste

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Intermediate

Number of Servings: 4 persons

Kcal per Serving: Approximately 650 kcal

Preparation Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil before gently adding the fettuccine. Cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a deep pan, heat the extra virgin olive oil over medium-high heat. Saute the sliced mushrooms and minced garlic until the mushrooms are tender and golden brown.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium, and melt the unsalted butter among the mushrooms. Once bubbling, pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Gradually stir in the grated parmesan cheese until it fully melts into the sauce. Season with sea salt and a pinch of ground nutmeg.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the pan, tossing it gently to ensure each strand is lovingly coated in the creamy sauce.
  6. Simmer together for another 2 minutes, then remove from the heat. Incorporate the chopped fresh basil leaves for a pop of color and freshness.
  7. Plate up the velouté, garnishing with more basil or parmesan as desired.

Tip: For an added gourmet twist, drizzle with a touch of truffle oil before serving, or sprinkle over toasted pine nuts for a delightful crunch. For a non-vegetarian version, consider adding crispy pancetta or sautéed shrimp to elevate this dish further.
