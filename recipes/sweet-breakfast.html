Sweet Breakfast of the day

Bountiful Breakfast Bliss

Indulge in Morning Splendor

Start your day on a luxurious note with this exquisite gourmet recipe, Bountiful Breakfast Bliss. Immerse yourself in the sumptuous taste of velvety chocolate spread lovingly enveloped by feather-light crepes. Ideal for leisurely weekend mornings, this dish is a celebration of refined flavors and comforting textures, guaranteed to elevate your breakfast experience to new heights.

Working Time: 30 minutes
Cooking/Baking Time: 10 minutes
Resting Time: 1 hour
Difficulty: Intermediate
Number of Servings: 4
Calories per Serving: ~650 kcal

Ingredients:

  • 125g all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 250 ml milk
  • 30g unsalted butter (melted, plus extra for cooking)
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • A pinch of sea salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 100g hazelnuts (toasted and ground, for garnish)
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 200 ml heavy cream (plus extra for the chocolate spread)

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, eggs, milk, melted butter, sugar, salt, and vanilla extract until you have a smooth batter. Let it rest for 1 hour at room temperature.
  2. For the chocolate spread, heat 200 ml of heavy cream in a small saucepan until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat and whisk in the cocoa powder until smooth. Set aside to cool.
  3. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and lightly coat with butter. Pour in a small amount of crepe batter, swirling to spread evenly. Cook for about 1-2 minutes per side, or until golden. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  4. Lay out the crepes and spread each with a generous layer of chocolate spread. Roll or fold as desired.
  5. Whip the additional heavy cream until stiff peaks form, and serve alongside the crepes for added indulgence.
  6. Garnish the crepes with toasted, ground hazelnuts for a nutty crunch and an extra touch of elegance.

Tips to Vary or Improve:

  • Swap out hazelnuts for slivered almonds or crushed pistachios for a different twist on the nutty garnish.
  • Add a dash of cinnamon or orange zest into the crepe batter for a subtle flavor twist.
  • Serve with slices of banana or strawberries for a fruity addition that complements the rich chocolate.
  • To turn it into a vegan delight, use plant-based milk, vegan butter, and a dairy-free cream alternative for the spread and whipped cream. Use egg substitutes for binding the crepe batter.

Remember, the key to a divine Bountiful Breakfast Bliss is patience and the love poured into the preparation. Enjoying this with a freshly brewed cup of artisan coffee or a floral tea can amplify the gourmet experience.
