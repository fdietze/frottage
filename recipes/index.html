Sweet Breakfast of the day

Blissful Breakfast Bites

Captivate Your Morning Cravings

Start your day with a harmonious harmony of sweet and nutritious flavors! Our Blissful Breakfast Bites are a gourmet‚Äôs dream, exquisitely crafted with a pinch of hipster flair. Perfect for leisurely mornings or an upscale brunch with friends, these bites will enchant your palate and nourish your soul. With a working time of mere minutes, create a masterpiece that looks as divine as it tastes.

Prep Time

  • Working Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Resting Time: N/A
  • Total Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty

  • Easy

Servings

  • Makes 4 servings

Caloric Content

  • Approx. 350 kcal per serving

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon, 2 teaspoons
  • Vanilla bean, 1, scraped
  • Maple syrup, 4 tablespoons
  • Fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries), 150 grams of each
  • Sliced almonds, 50 grams
  • Greek yogurt, 250 grams
  • Honey, 2 tablespoons
  • Whole grain bread, 4 thick slices
  • Mascarpone cheese, 100 grams
  • Bananas, 2, sliced

Instructions

  1. Toast the whole grain bread slices until they are golden and crisp.
  2. While the bread is toasting, in a small bowl, mix Greek yogurt with vanilla bean scrapings and a tablespoon of honey to create your vanilla-infused yogurt.
  3. In another bowl, gently mix the fresh berries with a teaspoon of cinnamon and a tablespoon of maple syrup for a zesty, sweet berry compote.
  4. Spread a generous layer of mascarpone cheese onto each slice of the toasted bread.
  5. Arrange the sliced bananas over the mascarpone in an even layer.
  6. Spoon the vanilla-infused Greek yogurt over the bananas, followed by a drizzling of honey.
  7. Top each bite with the cinnamon berry compote, ensuring a good mix of strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries.
  8. Finally, sprinkle sliced almonds over the bites for a satisfying crunch.
  9. Serve immediately with an extra drizzle of maple syrup for those with a sweet tooth.

Nutritional Tip

For an added fiber boost and a unique taste twist, sprinkle some chia seeds or flaxseeds over your Blissful Breakfast Bites. Endeavor into vegan territory by replacing Greek yogurt and mascarpone with plant-based alternatives, and maple syrup for honey. These simple swaps cater to dietary restrictions without sacrificing flavor.
