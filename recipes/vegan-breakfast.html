Vegan Breakfast of the day

Blissful Breakfast Ballet

Vibrant Vegan, Morning Magic

Introduction

Start your day on a high note with the "Blissful Breakfast Ballet" – a harmonious dance of energizing vegetables, delicate spices, and wholesome ingredients that nourish the soul and awaken the senses. Perfect for the discerning gourmet seeking an elegant, plant-based beginning to their morning routine.

Working Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 100 g baby spinach, fresh
  • 120 g chickpea flour
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp black salt (Kala Namak)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Resting Time: None

Difficulty: Medium

Number of Servings: 2 servings

kcal per Serving: Approx. 400 kcal

Preparation Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the chickpea flour, turmeric, minced garlic, black salt, and 240 ml of water to create a smooth batter. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour in half of the batter to form a thin pancake. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the edges lift easily, then flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Repeat with the remaining batter to make a second pancake.
  3. In another pan, sauté the red bell pepper in the remaining olive oil until tender and slightly charred. Add the baby spinach and cook until just wilted.
  4. To assemble, lay out each chickpea pancake on a plate. Top with wilted baby spinach, red bell peppers, diced tomatoes, and slices of avocado.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and a sprinkle of black salt if desired. Serve immediately for the freshest experience.

Gourmet Tip

For an extra zest, add a squeeze of lemon juice or a drizzle of balsamic glaze over the top of your Blissful Breakfast Ballet. To vary, consider swapping out baby spinach for arugula for a peppery kick, or add a dollop of vegan yogurt for a creamy contrast.
