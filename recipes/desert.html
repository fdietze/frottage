Desert of the day

Sandy Sweet Swirls

Delightfully Decadent, Whimsically Wrapped

Indulge in the whimsy of Sandy Sweet Swirls, a hipster gourmet dessert that intertwines the rustic warmth of cinnamon and the aromatic splendor of vanilla into a decadent, swirled delight. This dessert not only tantalizes the taste buds but also serves as a conversation starter on any hip foodie gathering table.

Working Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Cinnamon, 3 tsp
  • Vanilla beans, 2 pods (or 2 tsp vanilla extract)
  • Sugar, 150 g
  • All-purpose flour, 250 g
  • Unsalted butter, 150 g, room temperature
  • Eggs, 2 large
  • Heavy cream, 100 ml
  • Honey, 50 ml
  • Sea salt, 1 pinch
  • Almonds, 100 g (slivered)

Cooking/Baking Time: 20-25 minutes

Resting Time: 1 hour

Difficulty: Medium

Number of Servings: 12 swirls

Kcal per Serving: Approx. 280 kcal

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Split the vanilla beans lengthwise and scrape out the seeds. Mix the seeds or vanilla extract with the sugar to make vanilla sugar.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine flour and butter, rubbing them together until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  4. Stir in the vanilla sugar and cinnamon to the flour mixture.
  5. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, and honey until well combined.
  6. Slowly add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until a smooth dough forms.
  7. Fold in the slivered almonds and a pinch of sea salt.
  8. On a floured surface, roll the dough into a log and wrap it in cling film. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  9. Once chilled, cut the log into 12 equal slices.
  10. Place swirls onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the edges are a golden brown.
  12. Let the swirls cool on a wire rack before serving.

Tip: For an extra indulgent twist, drizzle melted dark chocolate over the swirls once cooled. You can also incorporate orange zest into the dough for a citric nuance or swap almonds for pecans for a different nutty undertone. Pair with a freshly brewed cup of artisanal coffee or a glass of dessert wine to elevate the dessert experience even further.
