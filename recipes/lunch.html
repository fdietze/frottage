Luminous Luncheon Lift

Refresh, relish, recharge, rejuvenate.

A symphony of garden-fresh ingredients coalescing into a burst of midday verve, the Luminous Luncheon Lift is the epitome of hipster gastronomy. Constructed with a medley of organic baby greens, sun-kissed cherry tomatoes, and a host of summery flavors, this dish promises a tasteful retreat from the hustle of your avant-garde lifestyle.

Working Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 150g mixed baby greens
  • 200g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 medium cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 100g feta cheese, crumbled
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 tsp cracked black pepper, or to taste

Cooking/Baking Time: None

Resting Time: None

Difficulty: Easy

Number of Servings: 4

Kcal per Serving: Approximately 200 kcal

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a large, aesthetically pleasing serving bowl, gently toss the mixed baby greens with the cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and julienned red bell pepper.
  2. Sprinkle the torn basil leaves and crumbled feta cheese over the top, distributing the ingredients evenly for a mosaic of colors and textures.
  3. In a small jar, combine the zest of one lemon, the juice of half a lemon, and extra virgin olive oil. Secure the lid and shake vigorously to emulsify the dressing.
  4. Drizzle the lemon vinaigrette over the salad, sprinkle with sea salt and cracked black pepper, and toss the salad once more to ensure the dressing coats every leaf and slice.
  5. Present the salad immediately, to maintain the crispness of the greens and the freshness of the flavors.

Tip: For a bohemian twist, add toasted pine nuts or pumpkin seeds for an earthy crunch. If you're aiming for a more substantial hipster feast, consider topping your salad with a soft-boiled, free-range egg or slices of avocado for a creamier texture and a boost of healthy fats.
