Vegetarian Lunch of the day

Verdant Velvet Varnish

Elevate Your Greens Gourmet

Discover a symphony of flavors with this hipster gourmet recipe, where every bite offers a medley of fresh textures and vibrant colors. Perfect for a midday uplift or a chic dinner starter, this dish celebrates the joy of healthy indulgence.

Preparation Time: 20 minutes
Cooking Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 35 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Servings: 4
Calories per serving: ~400 kcal

Ingredients:

  • 150g baby spinach leaves
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 150g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
  • 60g Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 200g quinoa
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves, roughly torn
  • 30ml extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Steps:

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water using a fine mesh strainer. Transfer to a medium saucepan and add 500ml of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until the quinoa is fluffy and water is absorbed.

  2. While the quinoa is cooking, prepare the vegetables. Wash the baby spinach and lay it as a base on the serving platter.

  3. Slice the avocado and halve the cherry tomatoes. Julienne the red bell pepper into thin strips.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, crushed garlic, and season with salt and pepper to create the dressing.

  5. Arrange the avocado slices, cherry tomatoes, red bell pepper strips, and Kalamata olives on top of the spinach leaves.

  6. Once the quinoa is cooked, let it cool for a few minutes then fluff it with a fork. Scatter it over the salad.

  7. Drizzle the dressing generously over the salad and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

  8. Toss gently before serving to make sure every portion is infused with the flavors of the dressing.

Difficulty:

Easy

Tip to vary or improve the recipe:

For an additional protein boost, add chickpeas or feta cheese to the salad. To add a crunch, sprinkle toasted pine nuts or pumpkin seeds on top before serving. If you're feeling adventurous, a dash of balsamic reduction drizzled over the salad will introduce a sweet and tangy twist. Enjoy this delightful fusion of health and flavor, where every forkful promises a burst of gourmet bliss.
