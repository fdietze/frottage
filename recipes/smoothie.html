Smoothie of the day

Blissful Blend Burst

Indulge in Velvety Vegan Virtue

Treat yourself to this luscious concoction, the Blissful Blend Burst—a smoothie that intertwines the creaminess of coconut yogurt with the sweetness of mangoes and dates, balanced with a hint of spice from fresh ginger and cinnamon. Every sip delivers a velvety texture packed with nutrients from the wholesome spinach and chia seeds, cradled in the nutty embrace of almond milk. Whether you’re kick-starting your morning or needing a midday pick-me-up, this gourmet smoothie is delightfully satisfying.

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Blending Time: 2 minutes

Total Time: 12 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Servings: 2

Calories per serving: ~350 kcal

Ingredients

  • Bananas: 2 (medium-sized, ripe)
  • Spinach: 100 g (fresh)
  • Almond milk: 250 ml
  • Chia seeds: 2 tablespoons
  • Mango: 1 (large, ripe)
  • Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Cinnamon: ½ teaspoon
  • Medjool dates: 4 (pitted)
  • Fresh ginger: 2 cm (peeled and finely grated)
  • Coconut yogurt: 150 g

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Peel the bananas, wash the spinach leaves, peel and cube the mango, pit the dates, and grate the ginger.
  2. Place the bananas, spinach, mango, dates, and ginger in a high-powered blender.
  3. Add the dry ingredients: chia seeds and cinnamon.
  4. Pour the almond milk and add a dollop of coconut yogurt for creaminess.
  5. Drizzle the vanilla extract over the mix.
  6. Blend on high speed for 2 minutes or until the smoothie reaches a smooth, creamy consistency.
  7. Taste and adjust for sweetness or spice, if necessary.
  8. Pour the smoothie into two glasses and serve fresh.

Tip: For an extra protein boost, add a scoop of your favorite plant-based protein powder. To vary the flavor, try swapping mango for peach or apricot in summer, or pear in autumn for a seasonal twist. For a colder beverage, use frozen banana slices and mango chunks.
