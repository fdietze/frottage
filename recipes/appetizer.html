Appetizer of the day

Savory Sunset Swirls

Entice with every twist

Escape into an evening of flavor elegance with these delectable Savory Sunset Swirls. These gourmet appetizers perfectly blend the tang of balsamic, the zest of cherry tomatoes, and the aromatic allure of basil encased in a delicate puff pastry. Ideal as a conversation-starter for your next hipster gathering or sophisticated soiree.

Working Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 250g puff pastry
  • 100g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 20 basil leaves, finely chopped
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 30ml olive oil
  • 15ml balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 50g red onion, thinly sliced

Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes

Resting Time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Moderate

Number of Servings: 6

Kcal per Serving: Approximately 250 kcal

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (392°F).
  2. Lay out the puff pastry and evenly sprinkle with minced garlic, finely chopped basil leaves, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Distribute the sliced cherry tomatoes and red onion over the pastry.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, black pepper, and sea salt. Drizzle this mixture over the arranged ingredients on the puff pastry.
  5. Carefully roll the loaded pastry into a log, ensuring it's tight enough to hold the filling but not so tight that it tears.
  6. Slice the log into 2 cm thick swirls and place them flat on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the pastry is puffed up and golden.
  8. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving to let the flavors meld together beautifully.

Tip: For an even more hipster twist, try adding a sprinkle of edible flowers or a dash of truffle oil before baking. This will elevate the aroma and add an Instagram-worthy touch to your appetizer!

Enjoy your artfully crafted Savory Sunset Swirls!
