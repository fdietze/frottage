Appetizer of the day

Mystic Mushroom Morsels

Entice, Excite, Enjoy, Enchant

Embark on a culinary journey with "Mystic Mushroom Morsels," the perfect prelude to any hipster gourmet meal. These savory bites combine the earthy tones of Portobello with the tangy embrace of goat and Parmesan cheese, all infused with the aromatic whispers of thyme, garlic, and parsley. This appetizer promises to tantalize your taste buds, delivering an exquisite blend of textures and flavors in every bite.

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cooking Time: 25 minutes
Resting Time: 5 minutes
Difficulty: Intermediate
Servings: 4
Calories per serving: Approximately 250 kcal

Ingredients:

  • Portobello mushrooms, 4 large caps
  • Goat cheese, 100 grams
  • Parmesan cheese, 50 grams (finely grated)
  • Fresh thyme, 1 tablespoon (chopped)
  • Garlic cloves, 2 (minced)
  • Fresh parsley, 1 tablespoon (chopped)
  • Bread crumbs, 100 grams
  • Olive oil, 3 tablespoons
  • Sea salt, to taste
  • Ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (356°F).
  2. Gently wipe the Portobello mushrooms with a damp cloth, and carefully remove their stems.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the goat cheese, half of the Parmesan, thyme, garlic, and parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Stuff the mixture into the mushroom caps and sprinkle them with bread crumbs followed by the remaining Parmesan cheese.
  5. Drizzle the stuffed mushrooms with olive oil and place them on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25 minutes, or until the tops are golden brown and the mushrooms are tender.
  7. Allow the "Mystic Mushroom Morsels" to rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Difficulty: Intermediate
Servings: 4
Kcal per serving: Approximately 250 kcal

Tip:

Enhance these "Mystic Mushroom Morsels" by adding a drizzle of truffle oil before serving, for an extra touch of gourmet luxury. Alternatively, experiment by incorporating finely chopped nuts such as walnuts or pecans into the stuffing mixture for an added crunch and depth of flavor.
