Savory Breakfast of the day

Sunrise Symphony

Awaken with Artisanal Aroma

Introduction: Begin your morning with a burst of flavor and color with Sunrise Symphony, a hipster gourmet recipe designed for the discerning palate. This delightful breakfast dish showcases baked eggs cradled in a lush bed of spinach, enriched with feta cheese and elevated by a medley of fresh herbs. A dish that's as beautiful to behold as it is to eat, it will have you dancing to the beat of the rising sun.

Work Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Fresh spinach – 250g
  • Large eggs – 4
  • Feta cheese – 100g, crumbled
  • Garlic – 2 cloves, minced
  • Olive oil – 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh dill – 2 tablespoons, chopped
  • Fresh parsley – 2 tablespoons, chopped
  • Black pepper – a pinch, freshly ground
  • Cherry tomatoes – 150g, halved
  • Red onion – 1 small, thinly sliced

Cooking Time: 18-20 minutes

Resting Time: 5 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Number of Servings: 4

Kcal per Serving: ~250 kcal

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (approximately 392°F).

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the sliced red onion and minced garlic until translucent, about 3 minutes.

  3. Add the spinach to the skillet and cook until just wilted. Stir in the cherry tomatoes until they're just heated through, then remove from heat.

  4. Add the fresh dill and parsley to the skillet, tossing everything to combine. Season with black pepper to taste.

  5. Transfer the spinach mixture to a greased baking dish, spreading it into an even layer.

  6. Make four wells in the spinach mixture with the back of a spoon. Crack an egg into each well and sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese around the eggs.

  7. Place the baking dish in the oven and bake for 18-20 minutes or until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny.

  8. Remove from the oven and let sit for 5 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.

Tip: Personalize your Sunrise Symphony by experimenting with different herbs like basil or chives for a new melody of flavors. For a spicier kick, add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes. To make this dish even more luxurious, drizzle a spoonful of truffle oil over the baked eggs right before serving. Enjoy the orchestration of flavors in every bite!
