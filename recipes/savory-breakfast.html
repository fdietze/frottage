Savory Breakfast of the day

Morning Majesty Munch

Begin with balance, bask in flavor

Introduction:

Embark on a culinary journey each morning with the "Morning Majesty Munch," a dish that intertwines the regal richness of farm-fresh eggs with a tapestry of garden-picked greens and jewel-toned vegetables. Each bite offers a symphony of flavors that are sure to awaken your palate and ensconce you in the comfort of gourmet simplicity. Perfect for the discerning hipster foodie, this recipe combines wholesome ingredients with artisanal charm.

Working Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs (approx. 220g)
  • 100g aged cheddar cheese, grated
  • 75g baby spinach leaves
  • 150g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 10 basil leaves, roughly torn
  • 1 medium sweet bell pepper (approx. 150g), diced
  • 1 tbsp chives, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Possibly Resting Time: N/A

Difficulty: Easy

Number of Servings: 2

Kcal per Serving: Approximately 350 kcal

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, crack the eggs and add sea salt, smoked paprika, and black pepper. Whisk until well combined and frothy.
  2. Place a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the whisked eggs to the pan. Allow them to set for a brief moment before gently stirring to create soft curds.
  3. While the eggs are still slightly runny, sprinkle in the grated cheddar cheese, allowing it to melt and integrate into the eggs.
  4. Add the baby spinach and diced sweet bell peppers, and cook for an additional 2â€“3 minutes until the spinach wilts and the eggs are cooked to your preference.
  5. Remove from heat and gently fold in the cherry tomatoes and torn basil leaves, preserving their fresh flavor and vibrant color.
  6. Garnish with chopped chives for an extra layer of flavor and a pop of color.
  7. Serve the Morning Majesty Munch immediately, splitting the contents evenly between two plates. Savor the harmony of wholesome ingredients and artisanal taste in your hip, gourmet breakfast.

Tip:

To elevate this recipe further, consider drizzling with a balsamic glaze or adding a dollop of herbed goat cheese for a tangy twist. For a heartier option, serve atop a toasted slice of sourdough bread, creating a canvas for this colorful, flavorsome masterpiece.
